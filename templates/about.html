About the project

Craft breweries often follow different philosophies of brewing - while some breweries specialise in particular beer styles, others are trying to keep their offer as diverse as possible.

Brewsearch is a simple wee app that allows you to find a brewery and its' most popular beer styles. As beer styles are very broadly defined, you can explore each one further by clicking on one of the chart's sections- this will allow you to see the specific substyles that each brewery is focusing on.

Note: The App uses Untappd's public API, meaning up to 50 most popular beers from each brewery can be displayed in the chart. Additionally, there is a possibility that 'Numer of beers' by each brewery is not correct as some collaboration beers might not be included in the total count.

All data is sourced from Untappd.
